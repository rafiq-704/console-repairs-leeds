How to get your device to us

Just pop over to our store

We are located in Horsforth Leeds, opposite the Enigma Bar on the A65. We have our own dedicated car park to the side of our building. We are open from Monday to Saturday from 9am to 6pm. If you need help locating our store or need any further information please call 0113 259 1082

By Post (Registered Post)

We currently offer a postal service. The device has to be sent recorded and tracking details emailed to info@consolerepairleeds.co.uk

Collect & Return (Leeds & Bradford only)

We currently offer a collect and return service, a standard additional charge will be added to the price of the repair. One of our technician or representative will collect and drop off your device to your door.

The Console Repair Centre - All faults are resolved - No Fix, No Fee

We are Yorkshire's premier console repair centre, our shop has been established since 1995, our engineers are highly experienced with repairing delicate electronics of any kind. We cater for the high end customer where only the best will do. We guarantee all of our work.
