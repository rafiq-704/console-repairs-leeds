F.A.Q.

For a free no obligation quote call us on 0113 259 1082

  • HOW QUICKLY CAN YOU REPAIR MY CONSOLE?

    We aim to have your console repaired the very same day, by keeping all of the parts in stock we are able to offer a repair service without the requirement for any advanced notice or booking. With more involving repairs such as water damage or physical damaged consoles the repair procedure can take longer, if you need specific time scale information, please call us on the main shop number and we will be pleased to provide you a more concrete answer.

  • DO YOU CHARGE A FEE IF YOU CAN'T FIX MY CONSOLE?

    We do not charge a check and test fee on any repairs unless you have been made aware upon booking your console with us, this is to cover the not inconsiderable time required to check and attempts to fix the device.

  • WHAT KIND OF WARRANTY DO I GET?

    We offer a 90 day warranty on all our repairs. Unless otherwise stated all repairs come with 90 day warranty. Any damage to any parts we fit will invalidate your warranty.

  • DO YOU HAVE A CAR PARK?

    We have a large and dedicated car park, so you will not incur any parking fee's or tickets. We believe this takes out a lot of the stress involved in using other repair outlets in the city centre.

  • WHERE CAN I FIND YOUR SHOP?

    2 Rose Terrace, Leeds LS18 4QA
    Tel: 0113 259 1082

  • DO YOU PROVIDE A MAIL-IN SERVICE?

    We now offer a mail in service, we can also dispatch our own courier to collect your device, we will soon have a section on our website explaining how you can send your device to us.

  • DO YOU CARRY OUT REPAIRS AT YOUR WORKSHOP OR IS THE WORK OUTSOURCED?

    We do not outsource any of our repairs, our workshop is the largest of its kind in the UK, with this kind of size we able to keep on hand a large and dedicated team that is familiar with most faults, this allows us to carry out all repairs at our state of the art workshop.

  • CAN I GET A VAT INVOICE WITH MY REPAIR?

    Absolutely, as a genuine VAT registered company a full VAT invoice is provided with all the repairs we conduct.
