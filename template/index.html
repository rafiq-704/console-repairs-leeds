Our Services

XBOX Series

Professional in-house repairs for all Xbox models, covering every common fault.

From power issues to HDMI faults and overheating, our technicians diagnose and repair Xbox consoles using professional tools and genuine-quality parts

PlayStation Series

Advanced PlayStation repairs using professional reflow equipment.

We repair all PlayStation models with issues such as flashing lights, disc drive failures, overheating, and GPU faults, delivering stable and lasting repair solutions.

Nintendo Consoles

Expert repairs for Nintendo consoles, from Wii to legacy systems.

Our team fixes both common and rare Nintendo faults including overheating, disc errors, power issues, and internal hardware failures with precision care.

Handheld Consoles

Specialist repairs for handheld consoles others won’t attempt.

From broken screens and battery faults to charging and board-level issues, we carefully restore handheld devices using expert techniques and delicate repair processes.

iPad Repairs

Professional iPad repairs following Apple-grade repair standards.

We repair cracked screens, battery problems, charging faults, and software issues across all iPad models, restoring smooth performance and everyday usability.

iPhone Repairs

Precision iPhone repairs using high-quality replacement components.

From screen and battery replacements to camera, speaker, and charging port repairs, we extend your iPhone’s life with fast and reliable local service.

Laptop Repairs

Comprehensive laptop repair services for all major brands.

We resolve hardware and software issues including screen damage, keyboard faults, overheating, slow performance, and power-related failures.

iMac Repairs

Specialist iMac repairs carried out by experienced technicians.

Our services include display repairs, power issues, system slowdowns, storage upgrades, and software fixes to restore peak iMac performance.

Computer Repairs

Reliable desktop computer repair solutions for home and business users.

We diagnose and fix slow systems, crashes, hardware failures, upgrade needs, and software problems to keep your computer running efficiently.
