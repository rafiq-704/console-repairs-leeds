Common Faults

Screen & Touch Issues

Common DS and 3DS screen faults include cracked LCDs, colour spots, flickering displays, or unresponsive touchscreens. We replace and repair both top and bottom screens to restore clear visuals and accurate touch response.

£24.99 – £39.99

Power & Charging Problems

If your console will not charge, drains power quickly, or switches off suddenly with a popping sound, the issue may be related to the battery or internal flex cables. We diagnose and repair all power-related faults.

£39.99 – £59.99

Game Cartridge Slot Faults

If your Nintendo DS or 3DS fails to read game cartridges, the cartridge slot may be dirty, damaged, or worn. We clean, repair, or replace cartridge readers to ensure games load correctly.

£29.99 – £39.99

Software & System Errors

Software-related issues such as corrupted digital game data or system errors can prevent normal operation. Where possible, we repair or restore software using official recovery methods and system tools.

£24.99 – £39.99

Button & Control Issues

Sticky buttons, unresponsive controls, or faulty analog sticks are common on DS and 3DS models. We repair or replace worn buttons and analog components to restore smooth gameplay.

£20.99 – £29.99

General Hardware Faults

Internal hardware faults can cause a variety of issues, including random shutdowns or system instability. Our technicians carry out detailed diagnostics to identify and repair underlying hardware problems.

£49.99 – £89.99
